The Time Line of Dr Garang's Life:

  • Garang was born in 23 June 1945 in Wangulei village, Twic, Sudan (now South Sudan).
  • He attended schools in Wau, Rumbek and then complete his secondary education in Tanzania.
  • In 1962 he joined the first Sudanese civil war.
  • After winning a scholarship, he went on to earn a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics in 1969 from Grinnell College in Iowa, United States.
  • In 1981 received a Master's degree in agricultural economics from Iowa State University.In 1983 he earned a PhD in Economics from Iowa State University.
  • In 1983 he become leader of SPLA/M army rebelling against the regime in Khartoum.
  • In 2005 he signed a comperhensive peace agreenment with Sudan government.
  • On July 9 2005 he was sworn as a vice president of Sudan.
  • On July 30 2005 he died at the age of 60 in a helicopter crash when he was returning after a meeting with President Yoweri Museveni of Uganda.
